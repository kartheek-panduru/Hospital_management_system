The ophthalmology department at Clinix Hospitals provides comprehensive care for a broad spectrum of eye diseases. The state-of-art diagnostic and surgical equipment, and sophisticated operation theatre make this department an advanced eye care center. Professional ophthalmologists and optometrists offer treatment from simple routine eye exams to complex surgical procedures. Our hospital has the top ophthalmologists in India who carefully identify and manage problems like cataracts, glaucoma, squint, retinal disorders, etc. They perform eye examinations, treat vision problems, and recommend fitting contact lenses and eyeglasses. The ophthalmology department has cutting-edge tools and methods for diagnosing and treating various complex and rare eye disorders. The department also features a full-fledged refractory surgery unit that provides services for LASIK, FEMTO LASIK, SMILE and ICL operative facilities. Some patients experience eye conditions due to other medical problems such as diabetes, immune system disorders, cardiovascular diseases, thyroid problems, and other health concerns. Our ophthalmologists understand their medical requirements and treat their eye problems accordingly.


Symptoms of eye diseases


  • Eye pain

  • Eye redness or swelling

  • Double vision

  • Blurred vision

  • Dry eyes

  • Burning eyes

  • Watery eyes

  • Lump on the eyelid

  • Drooping eyelid

  • Itching eyes

  • Eye irritation

  • Light sensitivity

  • Dilated pupil

  • Night blindness

  • Reduced vision
